Key Features of the Gym Membership System
Membership Types:

Normal Members: Fixed time slot access.
Gold Members: Two time slots and access to a personalized trainer.
Premium Members: Anytime access, two time slots, personalized trainer, and receive a protein box.
Time Slot Assignment:

Members are assigned a time slot during which they can access the gym. Gold members can select two slots.
Subscription Plans:

Members can choose from monthly, quarterly, or yearly subscriptions.
Gym Rules:

The gym is closed on Sundays.
Premium members receive a protein box monthly.
